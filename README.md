
  # Cyberpunk Food Festival Prototype

  Taste the Future in Neon 
UI/UX Case Study – IIIT-B Designathon 
Designer: Pixel Protomasters 
Tools Used: Figma, FigJam, Photoshop 
Problem Statement 
Visitors faced long queues, scattered ordering options, and poor coordination among food 
stalls during the Cyberpunk Food Festival. They missed offers and lacked a unified digital 
experience. 
Objective 
To design a unified, futuristic ordering app that makes food discovery, ordering, and 
engagement effortless while maintaining a cyberpunk theme. 
Target Users 
User 
Students 
Staff 
Guests 
Needs 
Quick ordering, coupon 
deals 
Simple checkout 
Easy stall access 
Pain Points 
Long queues 
Scattered info 
No clear interface 
UX Design Process 
Empathize → De ine → Ideate → Prototype → Test 
Research revealed pain points in ordering. Wireframes and prototypes were created and 
refined using user feedback. 
Key Features 
 QR-based Stall Ordering – Scan code to access menus instantly. 
 Live Order Tracker – Glowing neon progress bar shows real-time updates. 
 Coupon Wallet – Gamified offers like “Unlock a Neon Combo.” 
 Theme Switcher – Toggle between Neon Night and Holographic Day. 
 Festival Leaderboard – Top dishes and coupon redeemers. 
 AR Food Preview – Optional holographic 3D preview of dishes. 
 Voice ordering & modulation 
 Popular festival foods section 
 Smart seat detection (low-crowd zones) 
 Gen-Z captions & hashtags for IG sharing 
 Dynamic animations for a festival vibe 
Design System 
• Palette: Neon Pink (#FF00FF), Cyan (#00FFFF), Deep Black (#0A0A0A) 
• Typography: JetBrains Mono (Headings), Inter (Body) 
• Components: Glowing buttons, rounded cards, progress bars 
Impact 
60% reduction in queue time (estimated) 
85% users preferred QR ordering 
Higher engagement from gamified coupons
